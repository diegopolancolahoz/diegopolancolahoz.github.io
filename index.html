Welcome

Hello! I’m Diego A. Polanco-Lahoz, a Ph.D. candidate at Texas Tech University in Systems and Engineering Management. My academic research, supported by the National Science Foundation, focuses on assessing organizational resilience in higher education institutions using advanced methods such as RAG and FRAM. I am also passionate about engineering education, identity development in doctoral students, and practical applications of human factors and process improvement methodologies.

This site serves as a portfolio of my academic journey, research, teaching, and professional contributions. Feel free to explore and contact me with any inquiries or collaboration ideas.
