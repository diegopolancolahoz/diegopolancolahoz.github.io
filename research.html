Research

My research interests center around organizational resilience, systems engineering, and the design of educational systems. I investigate how institutions—particularly universities—can prepare for, respond to, and grow from complex challenges.

Dissertation Focus

Leveraging Resilience Analysis Grid (RAG) and Functional Resonance Analysis Method (FRAM) for Assessing Organizational Resilience Potential in Higher Education Institutions

This work, supported by the National Science Foundation, integrates systems thinking with institutional assessment to understand resilience at the programmatic and organizational level in academia.

Methodologies

  • Systematic Literature Review (SLR)
  • User Experience (UX) Research Methods
  • Survey Design and Statistical Analysis
  • Journey Mapping and Identity Modeling
  • Functional Modeling (FRAM) and Resilience Assessment (RAG)
