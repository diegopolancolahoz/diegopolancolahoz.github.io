Hello! I’m Diego A. Polanco-Lahoz, a Ph.D. candidate in Systems and Engineering Management at Texas Tech University. My research interests span organizational resilience, engineering education, and human factors in complex systems. I am particularly focused on improving processes in higher education institutions using innovative methods like the Resilience Analysis Grid (RAG) and Functional Resonance Analysis Method (FRAM).

Before coming to Texas Tech, I earned both my B.S. and M.S. in Industrial Engineering from Pontificia Universidad Católica de Valparaíso in Chile. I have also worked as a business analyst and served as a teaching and research assistant in multiple roles. I’m passionate about making research practical, collaborative, and globally relevant.
